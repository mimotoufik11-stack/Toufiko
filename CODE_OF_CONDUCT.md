# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

The Salafi Science Network is committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive community for all participants. We are dedicated to creating an environment where everyone feels safe, respected, and valued, regardless of background, experience level, or any other characteristic.

## Our Standards

We expect all community members to:

### Positive Behaviors

- **Be Respectful**: Treat all participants with dignity and respect
- **Be Inclusive**: Welcome people of all backgrounds and experiences
- **Be Constructive**: Provide helpful feedback and contributions
- **Be Transparent**: Communicate clearly and honestly
- **Be Accountable**: Take responsibility for your words and actions
- **Focus on Content**: Discuss ideas, not personalities
- **Be Patient**: Remember we're all learning and growing

### Islamic Values

As a community centered on Islamic knowledge, we uphold:

- **Adab (Etiquette)**: Respectful and courteous behavior
- **Sincerity**: Genuine intention to share and learn authentic knowledge
- **Humility**: Recognition that knowledge comes from continued learning
- **Justice**: Fair treatment of all community members
- **Compassion**: Kindness and understanding toward others

## Prohibited Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- **Harassment**: Unwelcome attention, threats, or intimidation
- **Discrimination**: Treatment based on protected characteristics
- **Hate Speech**: Language that demeans or attacks individuals or groups
- **Abuse**: Insulting, demeaning, or hostile language
- **Misinformation**: Deliberately spreading false or misleading information
- **Spam**: Unsolicited or repetitive commercial messages
- **Trolling**: Deliberately provocative behavior to disrupt community
- **Privacy Violations**: Sharing others' personal information without consent
- **Exploitation**: Taking advantage of community members

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- GitHub issues and pull requests
- Discussion forums
- Comments on content
- Social media posts related to this project
- Any official project communication channels

## Reporting Issues

If you witness or experience behavior that violates this Code of Conduct:

1. **Document**: Note the specific behavior and context
2. **Report**: Contact the project maintainers with details
3. **Evidence**: Include links, timestamps, or screenshots if possible
4. **Confidentiality**: Your report will be handled confidentially

### How to Report

- **Email**: Contact project maintainers privately
- **GitHub**: File a confidential issue (if available)
- **Direct Message**: Contact maintainers on appropriate platform

## Investigation and Response

The project maintainers will:

1. **Acknowledge** receipt of your report promptly
2. **Investigate** the situation thoroughly and fairly
3. **Communicate** findings to involved parties as appropriate
4. **Take Action** based on severity and circumstances
5. **Follow Up** to ensure resolution

## Enforcement

Appropriate responses to violations may include:

- **Warning**: For minor first-time violations
- **Temporary Restriction**: Limited participation while issues are resolved
- **Permanent Ban**: For severe or repeated violations

Decisions will be made fairly, considering:

- Severity of the violation
- Pattern of behavior
- Intent and context
- Willingness to improve

## Appeals

If you believe a decision was unfair:

1. Request appeal from project maintainers
2. Provide additional context or information
3. A different maintainer will review your appeal
4. Final decision will be communicated

## Examples

### Acceptable Behavior

✅ "I respectfully disagree with this interpretation because..."  
✅ "This is helpful. Have you considered...?"  
✅ "I think there's an error in the source. Here's what I found..."  
✅ "Welcome to the community! Don't hesitate to ask questions."  
✅ "Thank you for sharing this perspective."

### Unacceptable Behavior

❌ "Your opinion is worthless and stupid."  
❌ "You're just a troll trying to spread misinformation."  
❌ "People like you don't belong here."  
❌ Sharing someone's personal information  
❌ Deliberately spreading false claims
