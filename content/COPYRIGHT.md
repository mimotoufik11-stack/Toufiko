# Copyright and Usage Restrictions

## Content Attribution

All content provided on the Salafi Science Network platform is compiled from authentic Islamic sources with proper attribution to original authors and scholars. The content includes:

- **Original Texts**: Direct quotes from classical scholars and their works
- **Contemporary Scholarship**: Modern explanations and applications by qualified scholars
- **Educational Summaries**: Condensed versions prepared by the Salafi Science Network Editorial Team

## Usage Permissions

### Permitted Uses
- **Personal Study**: Individuals may use content for personal Islamic education and worship
- **Educational Settings**: Teachers may use content in classroom settings with proper attribution
- **Non-Profit Distribution**: Content may be shared freely for non-commercial educational purposes
- **Translation**: Content may be translated into other languages with attribution to original sources

### Restricted Uses
- **Commercial Exploitation**: Content may not be sold or used for commercial profit without explicit permission
- **Modification**: Altering content to change Islamic meanings or scholarly positions is prohibited
- **Misrepresentation**: Content may not be attributed to incorrect sources or used to misrepresent scholarly positions
- **Plagiarism**: Using content without proper attribution to original authors and sources

## Scholarly Rights

The rights of the original scholars and their works are respected according to Islamic principles:

1. **Preservation of Intent**: Content is presented in a manner that preserves the original scholarly intent
2. **Contextual Accuracy**: Excerpts are used with proper context to avoid misinterpretation
3. **Scholarly Attribution**: All quotes and references are properly attributed to their sources
4. **Intellectual Property**: The intellectual property rights of scholars and publishers are respected

## Source Attribution Requirements

When using content from this platform, users must:

1. **Cite Original Sources**: Reference the original scholars and their works
2. **Include Platform Attribution**: Mention the Salafi Science Network as the compiler/translator when applicable
3. **Provide Links**: Include links to original sources when available
4. **Maintain Context**: Present content within its proper Islamic and scholarly context

## Disclaimer of Responsibility

The Salafi Science Network:

1. **Compilation Role**: Acts as a compiler and presenter of existing Islamic knowledge
2. **No Original Claims**: Does not claim original authorship of classical Islamic texts
3. **Educational Purpose**: Provides content solely for educational and spiritual benefit
4. **Scholarly Oversight**: All content is reviewed for authenticity and proper attribution

## Reporting Violations

Users who become aware of copyright violations or misuse of content should report them to ensure proper attribution and usage according to Islamic principles.

## Islamic Principles of Knowledge

This platform operates on the Islamic principle that knowledge is a trust (amanah) that should be:

- **Preserved**: Maintained in its authentic form
- **Shared**: Disseminated for the benefit of the Muslim community
- **Attributed**: Properly credited to its sources
- **Respected**: Treated with the dignity Islamic knowledge deserves

## Contact Information

For questions about usage rights, permissions, or to report violations, please contact the platform administrators through the official channels.
