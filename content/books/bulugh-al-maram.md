Bulugh al-Maram gathers evidences for jurisprudence, making it an essential reference for students and imams. This companion
offers thematic outlines, recommended commentaries, and memory aids for each chapter. It also highlights practical issues
for contemporary communities while staying faithful to scholarly explanations.
