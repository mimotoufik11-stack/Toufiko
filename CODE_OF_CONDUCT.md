# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

### Behavior that contributes to a positive environment

- **Respectful Communication**: Using welcoming and inclusive language
- **Constructive Feedback**: Providing and accepting constructive criticism graciously
- **Collaboration**: Focusing on what is best for the community
- **Empathy**: Showing empathy towards other community members
- **Patience**: Being patient with newcomers and those learning
- **Islamic Etiquette**: Adhering to Islamic principles in all interactions
- **Knowledge Sharing**: Willingness to help others learn and grow

### Unacceptable behavior

- **Harassment**: Use of sexualized language or imagery, unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- **Trolling**: Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or following
- **Disrespect**: Insulting/derogatory comments, personal or political attacks
- **Discrimination**: Any form of discrimination based on personal characteristics
- **Spam**: Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- **Disruption**: Any other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting
- **Un Islamic Content**: Content that contradicts authentic Islamic teachings

## Islamic Principles

As an Islamic educational platform, we expect all interactions to align with Islamic principles:

- **Honesty**: Be truthful in all communications
- **Kindness**: Speak gently and avoid harsh language
- **Patience**: Exercise patience in disagreements
- **Forgiveness**: Be quick to forgive mistakes
- **Respect**: Respect different opinions within Islamic bounds
- **Modesty**: Maintain appropriate language and behavior
- **Sincerity**: Act with sincere intentions to benefit others

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please:

1. **Immediate Response**: Respond calmly and professionally if you feel safe doing so
2. **Report**: Contact the project maintainers privately
3. **Document**: Keep records of the incident if possible

### Contact Information

- **Primary Contact**: [Maintainer Email]
- **GitHub Issues**: Create a private issue if needed
- **Emergency**: For serious incidents, contact GitHub support directly

### Investigation Process

1. **Receipt**: We will acknowledge receipt within 24 hours
2. **Investigation**: We will investigate the report thoroughly
3. **Action**: We will take appropriate action based on findings
4. **Follow-up**: We will follow up with all parties involved

### Consequences

Unacceptable behavior may result in:

- **Warning**: Private warning about inappropriate behavior
- **Temporary Suspension**: Temporary removal from community spaces
- **Permanent Ban**: Permanent expulsion from the community
- **Content Removal**: Removal of inappropriate content
- **Legal Action**: Involvement of law enforcement if necessary

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- **Project Spaces**: All project repositories, issues, and discussions
- **Public Spaces**: Public spaces when an individual is representing the project
- **Community Events**: Online and offline community events
- **Private Communications**: Direct messages related to the project

## Enforcement Guidelines

### Maintainer Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for:

- **Clarifying Standards**: Explaining and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior
- **Leading by Example**: Modeling positive behavior
- **Responsive Action**: Responding promptly to reports
- **Fair Treatment**: Treating all parties with respect and fairness
- **Documentation**: Documenting incidents and resolutions

### Conflict Resolution

1. **Mediation**: Attempt informal resolution when possible
2. **Escalation**: Escalate to senior maintainers if needed
3. **Documentation**: Document all steps taken
4. **Transparency**: Be transparent about process while respecting privacy
