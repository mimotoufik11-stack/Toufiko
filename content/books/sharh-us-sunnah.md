Sharh us-Sunnah by Imam al-Barbahari is a concise manual detailing the creed of Ahlus-Sunnah wal-Jamaa'ah. Students are
encouraged to memorise its principles, refer to the commentary of scholars such as Shaykh Salih al-Fawzan, and implement
the guidance in their lives.

This guide summarises the major themes, provides discussion prompts, and points to dependable audio explanations for
self-paced study circles.
